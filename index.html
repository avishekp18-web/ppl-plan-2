Quick Reminder
What is RPE?

RPE = Rate of Perceived Exertion. A 1–10 scale for how hard a set felt — how many reps you had left in the tank.

RPEFeelingReps left
6Easy4+ more
7Moderate3 more
8Challenging2 more
9Very hard1 more
10Max effortNothing left
How to use it: Pick a weight matching the week's RPE on your last rep. Bad sleep or low energy? Go lighter — that's the point.
